About The Event

IEEE SB TKMCE as a part of Phase 2 of Encamp 2.0 presents Project Cicada, a unique game designed to test your logic and tech-skills in cryptography.

The online event gives an oppurtunity to use your knowledge to decode your way through to solve the puzzle. Featuring 6 levels of unique puzzles for the player to decrypt through, the player that decrypt the fastest wins.

Exclusively for 1st years

So what are you waiting for? Get ready to bring your A-game and compete against other tech-savvy's.
